# Vibe Storefront Social Demo Design

## Style Prompt

Clean dark-green product-tech demo for a founder tool. The visual system should feel sharp, modern, and credible, with subtle electric motion that makes a raw idea feel like it is becoming a real product. Use restrained meme energy only in the product words, not in the art direction.

## Colors

- Background: `#061f18`
- Deep Canvas: `#04110e`
- Primary Teal: `#2be7b8`
- Warm Highlight: `#f4d35e`
- Soft White: `#f8fff9`
- Muted Text: `#9fb8ad`

## Typography

- Use Inter, ui-sans-serif, system-ui, and Arial.
- Headlines should be heavy, tight, and clear. Supporting copy should be medium weight with generous line height.

## Motion

- Fast first beat, then smooth controlled reveals.
- Use short vertical lifts, scale pops, and scan-line progress motion.
- No chaotic bounces, novelty type, sticker clutter, or large decorative blobs.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not make the canvas goofy, fantasy-themed, or arcade-heavy.
- Do not use purple-blue SaaS gradients.
- Do not rely on tiny text.
- Do not animate layout properties.
- Do not add visual noise that fights the app footage.
